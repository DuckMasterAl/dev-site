Sleep Reminder Privacy Policy

What information is stored?

Your status, userid, and user settings (Early Sleep and IDLE Mode) are stored.

Why we store the information and how we use it.

The status information is stored in order to provide the bot with sleep information to DM You about successfully sleeping yesterday, or to DM you about not sleeping on time.

Who gets this data?

Currently, this data is only shared with you and DuckMasterAl#0001, but this is subject to change.

How to Remove your data.

To remove your data, do the ,deleteaccount command. This will also remove your profile from our system.

Questions and Concerns.

If you have any Questions or Concerns about the data being stored, please DM DuckMasterAl#0001.


Note: We reserve the right to change this without notifying our users.
