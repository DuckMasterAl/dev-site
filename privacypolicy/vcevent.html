VC Moderator Privacy Policy

What information is stored?

No information (other than your userid on certain events) is stored.

Events include if you've used your afk timeout and Warning Data.

Why we store the information and how we use it.

This information is stored to keep the event running smoothly.

Who gets this data?

Your userid is public, but the specific user data can only be admins.

Questions and Concerns.

If you are questions or are concerned about the data stored by the bot please open a support ticket in Duckville via the !new command.

How to Remove your data.

If you would like your data to be removed please open a support ticket in Duckville via the !new command.

Warning Data will not be removed.


Note: We reserve the right to change this without notifying our users.
