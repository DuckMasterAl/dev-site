Duckville Manager Privacy Policy

What information is stored?

No information (other than your userid on certain events) is stored.

Events may include joining/leaving a Voice Channel, Updating your Status, and Updating your username/avatar.

Why we store the information and how we use it.

This information is stored for the following items:

  • The Game Reminder System
  • The Virus Event (When there is no active event, the data is not stored).
  • XP Farming Data.

Who gets this data?

Your userid is public, but the specific user data can only be seen by me, DuckMasterAl#0001

Questions and Concerns.

If you are questions or are concerned about the data stored by the bot please open a support ticket via the !new command.

How to Remove your data.

If you would like your data to be removed please open a support ticket via the !new command.

XP Farming Data will not be removed.


Note: We reserve the right to change this without notifying our users.
